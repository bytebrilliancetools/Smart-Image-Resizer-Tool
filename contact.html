Frequently Asked Questions

Is SmartImageResizer really free?

Yes! SmartImageResizer is completely free to use with no hidden charges or subscription fees.

Are my images stored on your servers?

No. All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

What file formats do you support?

We support JPG, PNG, WebP, BMP, and TIFF input formats. You can convert between JPEG, PNG, and WebP for output.

Is there a file size limit?

Since processing happens in your browser, there are no server-imposed file size limits. However, very large files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Can I use this tool for commercial projects?

Yes! You're free to use SmartImageResizer for both personal and commercial projects.

Do you offer batch processing?

Batch processing is currently in development and will be available in a future update.
